Quality

About Us

We are an “ISO 9001:2015” certified industry.

All our products pass through various Quality tests like;


•Spectrometer: to ensure chemical composition.
•Rockwell hardness tester to check the phyhsical property of rods.
• Fracture test of each rod.
• Final inspection to ensure that the entire finished product meets the quality guidelines before its packaging and dispatch.

We regularly upgrade our quality guidelines and review it periodically for its continuing suitability and make them compatible with the international quality standards.

Environment

We reap what we saw. At Venus Brassotech, we believe firmly in giving clean environment the first priority. All our plans, policies and processes are first scrutinized for their impact on the environment and then put into practice. We strive to conserve and even enhance natural resources. Furnace and heater hot gases are passed through bag houses where Zinc oxide is collected in bag house drums and oxide free gases are released at ambient temperature. Powder slag and oxides are then passed onto government authorised agencies for processing and clearance. The water effluents from the plant are managed and purified in a water treatment plant (ETP) before being reused and recycled.
