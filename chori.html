Who we are

The Khattar Group of Companies, founded in 1954, set up its first factory to manufacture brass components, catering to the electric and transport industries. Over the past 60 years, this group has diversified into various sectors.

Vishnu Crucibles Pvt. Ltd

A comapny that trades into crucibles, required for melting alloy.

Khattar Marine Services Pvt. Ltd

They offer services like marine transportation, stevedoring, ship breaking and provisioning of tugs.

Saffron Enterprises Pvt. Ltd

They are sole distributors for Madura Garments for Peter England across the state of Gujarat since 2000 and are the biggest corporate suppliers of Madura Garments brand pan India.

This includes supplies to more than 100 outlets across Gujarat with multiple retail stores across the state. Safforn Enterprises Pvt. Ltd. is Corporate / Institute agents for all the Madura Garments Brands (Peter England, Van Heusen, Louis Phillippe, Allen Solly & Byford) pan India.

Venus Developers

Venus Brassotech is the latest endeavor by the Khattar Group, in the same industry the group started from in 1954. Founded formally in 2013, but drawing its lineage all the way down from 1954, Venus Brassotech extrudes high quality brass rods that meet the parameters of various Indian as well as International Standards.

Venus Brassotech, is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company, which is committed to providing quality products, services and value to our customers by creating an efficacy in manufacturing and delivery. Through excellence in its people, consistent quality and meticulous executions, we are providing raw materials to clients in various sectors such as auto components, plumbing, electric and agriculture.

We are the pioneers in the realm of environment friendly, Lead Free brass, which constitutes 80% of our total production.
