Focus on Quality

A culture that's quality-focused produces excellent products, delivers great customer service, takes care of its employees, and overall has a high bar of excellence across the entire organization.

Cost efficient

Our products are competitively priced as we always strive to make optimum use of our resources, while assuring sustainability.

Timely Delivery

A team of dedicated and well qualified engineers ensure that our customers get delivery of products at the right time.

Leadfree Solutions

Products made from Lead-free alloys are in accordance to “Reduction of Lead in Drinking Water Act” and are ideal for potable and industrial applications.

Low MOQ

We can supply a batch as low as 20 kgs, making sure that the quality of the product is of the highest standards.

Recycling

Recycling scrap metal helps to fulfill both a financial incentive and an environmental imperative. We process turnings/chips for removal of oil before we can recycle them responsibly.
